Privacy Policy for ChatCraft.org

Effective Date: Jan 29, 2024

Welcome to ChatCraft.org. Your privacy is crucial to us, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

While using ChatCraft.org, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Here is how we handle your data:

Chat Data

  • Storage: All chats are stored locally within your browser. This means your conversations are private and not accessible to us unless you choose to share them.
  • Sharing: If you decide to share your chat history, you will be prompted to log in via Google or GitHub. Upon login, your username and the chat content you choose to share will be stored on ChatCraft.org servers.

Large Language Model (LLM) Tokens

  • Storage: LLM tokens, necessary for the operation of our chat services, are also stored locally within your browser. These tokens are not transmitted to our servers.

Login Information

  • OAuth Login: For sharing chat history, we use OAuth login services provided by Google and GitHub. We collect your username from these services for the purpose of identifying your shared chats on our servers.

Use of Data

The data we collect from you is used in the following ways:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features of our service when you choose to do so
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our service
  • To monitor the usage of our service
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
